Pay the Dartford Crossing charge (Dart Charge)

Sign in to your Dart Charge account to pay or use this service to:

  • pay for a one-off crossing online
  • pay a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN)
  • set up an account
  • apply for a local resident discount account
  • check how many crossings you’ve paid for

Start now

Before you start

The crossing charge depends on your vehicle and account type.

Charges apply between 6am and 10pm every day, including Christmas and other bank holidays.

You must pay by midnight the day after you cross.

You can get a resident’s discount if you live in Thurrock or Dartford.

Other ways to pay

You can:

  • set up an account or apply for a local resident discount account by post
  • pay with cash at a payzone outlet
  • pay in advance or top-up a Dart Charge account by post
  • pay or set up an account by phone
  • convert an old Dart Tag account into a Dart Charge account by phone - you can no longer do this online
